About us

Generations of Tandoori Cooking


Taj Indian Restaurant in Huskisson has been a popular dining experience for more than 20 years and continues to attract lovers of real Indian food from far and wide. We have many more dishes to chose from whether you are vegetarian or non-vegetarian. All of our dishes are enhanced with our secret india based spice recipe. Taj Indian Restaurant offers you a selection of authentic North Indian dishes which you can request as mild, medium or hot that will excite your taste buds and make you want to come back for more ! We pride ourselves on having the highest quality of Indian food around and yet very reasonably priced. So Come to Taj Indian Restaurant and enjoy the ultimate experience in fine Indian dining.
